Common Questions

What data sources does SeasonAlpha use?

We utilize decades of high-quality, adjusted End-of-Day (EOD) historical price data from leading, reputable financial data providers, covering major US stocks and ETFs. Data integrity is a top priority.

Is seasonality guaranteed to work?

No trading strategy offers guaranteed profits. Seasonality identifies historical probabilities and tendencies based on statistical analysis. While this provides a potential edge by highlighting periods where odds may have been historically favorable, past performance does not guarantee future results. Always use proper risk management.

How does the 'Best Window Finder' work?

For your chosen stock and a specific duration (e.g., 10 trading days), the AI analyzes every possible overlapping 10-day window throughout the year across decades of historical data. It calculates key metrics like average return, win rate (% of positive periods), and a combined Seasonality Score for each window, then ranks them to highlight the periods with the statistically strongest historical performance bias (positive or negative).

What happens in the Expert Trading Sessions?

Included in the Elite plan, these are live online webinars hosted multiple times a week by experienced market practitioners. They typically discuss current market context, analyze potential seasonal setups found using SeasonAlpha in real-time, cover practical trade management techniques, risk considerations, and answer member questions interactively. Recordings are usually available for later viewing.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, absolutely. Both monthly and annual subscriptions can be managed or canceled at any time directly from your account settings page. If you cancel, your access will continue until the end of your current paid billing period.
